Privacy Policy

Simple Archery Score Pad does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including scores, round details, and personal details such as your name or club — is saved exclusively to your device's local storage and never transmitted to any server or third party.

Your data belongs to you. You are free to export, back up, delete, or transfer it at any time using the tools provided within the app.

We have no access to your data, and we never will.
